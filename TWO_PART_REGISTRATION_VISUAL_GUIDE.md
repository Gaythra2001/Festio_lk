## Color Scheme

- **Primary (Purple):** `#667eea` - Buttons, accents, selected states
- **Secondary (Dark Purple):** `#764ba2` - Gradients, backgrounds
- **Background (Dark):** `#0F1729` - Primary background
- **Background (Darker):** `#1A1F3A` - Secondary background
- **Text (Primary):** White - Main text
- **Text (Secondary):** `rgba(255,255,255,0.7)` - Secondary text
- **Text (Tertiary):** `rgba(255,255,255,0.4)` - Disabled/hint text
- **Success (Green):** `#10B981` - Status indicators
- **Error (Red):** `#EF4444` - Error messages

## Animations & Transitions

1. **Type Selection Cards:** Slide in with staggered delay
2. **User Registration:** Fade in smoothly
3. **Organizer Pages:** Slide left/right when changing pages
4. **Progress Bar:** Smooth linear animation as steps progress
5. **Buttons:** Scale and elevation effects on tap
6. **Error Messages:** Slide up with fade in

## Responsive Design

- **Mobile:** Optimized for small screens (< 6 inches)
- **Tablet:** Works on larger screens with proper spacing
- **Web:** Can be adapted for browser use
- **Landscape:** Single-column layout maintained
